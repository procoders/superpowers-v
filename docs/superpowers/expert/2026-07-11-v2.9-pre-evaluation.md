# Phase 1B — Domain Advisory: v2.9 Pre-Evaluation (DevEx / triage)

Binding domain constraints. Full KB: `docs/superpowers/expert/_knowledge-base/dev-workflow-triage-devex.md`.

## Design constraints (binding)
- **DC-1:** the fast-path offer must NOT become a per-request rubber-stamp (Anthropic telemetry:
  ~93% approval → reflexive "y-spamming" = de-facto silent auto-route, violating Iron-Invariant #4).
  Fold the offer into the ONE recon/clarify interaction; never a standalone screen. AC-11's
  remember-my-choice (per-category, explicit, revocable) is the fatigue mitigation.
- **DC-2:** the deterministic tiers must resolve inside the ~1s flow-of-thought ceiling (Nielsen);
  the T3 `light` call is the only latency risk — keep it off the common path, show a "checking…"
  affordance if it can exceed ~1s.
- **DC-3:** impact is decided on what a change semantically IS, not only where its file lives — hence
  the taxonomy's **content-pattern** side.
- **DC-4:** escalate on the diff as early as it allows; frame as "the safety net worked," not "we
  were wrong." A false-negative (shipped a bug) corrodes trust far more than a false-positive.
- **DC-5:** reversibility/blast-radius is a fail-closed OVERRIDE, not a third score axis. Two axes
  (difficulty × impact) is right; do not over-axis.

## High-impact taxonomy additions (content-pattern side — tiny diffs, big blast radius)
1. **Legal/compliance copy** — ToS, disclaimers, consent strings; a one-word consent edit can be a
   GDPR change.
2. **i18n interpolation placeholders** (`{{var}}`/`%s`) — deletion crashes/garbles across locales.
3. **Feature-flag definitions / default flips** — one line, global blast radius (real 2025 outages).
4. **Config/constant literals** — timeouts, pricing, quotas, rate limits (top outage class).
5. **shared_token / a11y constructs** — a "cosmetic" color that's a brand/contrast-compliance surface;
   `aria-label` overrides native naming and silently breaks WCAG.

## Risks to track
- R1 offer-fatigue defeating the honesty invariant (→ DC-1 + remember-my-choice).
- R2 false-positive noise → distrust of the whole gate (→ conservative-max + AC-12 precision in
  `/v:status` so drift is visible).
- R3 late escalation feeling worse than never offering (→ DC-4 framing).
