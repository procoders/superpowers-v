One command climbs all four: /v:epic

Most of the 17 /v:* commands are turn-based tools. /v:epic is the one that ladders up an autonomy level with each flag you add.

The through-line: Compound V covers all four, but the value sits in the two autonomous ones — the evaluator (02) and the judge (04) are cross-vendor and git-derived, not a single model grading itself, and every loop carries a real stop — a human gate or a circuit breaker — never a blind autopilot.
